What is digital accessibility?

Process of removing and preventing barriers to access so everyone can enjoy your work equally.

Physical

Muscle control limitations, joint disorders, missing limbs

Auditory

Partial or complete loss of hearing in one or both ears

Visual

Partial vision loss ("low vision"), or complete loss ("blindness"). Also includes people with reduced or increased sensitivity to certain colors ("color blindness").

Cognitive

Cognitive, learning, and neurological disorders including neurodiversity, neurological, behavioral, and mental health disorders.

Temporary

Broken limbs, vertigo or fatigue after medical procedures, recovery from accidents, undergoing physical theraphy.

Situational

High-stress environments like disaster/accident scenarios, distraction, broken trackpad/mouse forcing keyboard use.
